Root Beer Float Recipe



After dinner there is no better way to end the eating experience then with desert!
This is a very easy recipe that will be sure to impress your guests and be a fan favorite.

Materials Needed

    1 Pint of Vanilla Ice Cream
    1 Liter of Root Beer
    Large Glass or Bowl

Steps

  1. Prepare Glass For Ice Cream
  2. 3 Scoops of Vanilla Ice Cream
  3. Pour Rootbeer to Desired Level
