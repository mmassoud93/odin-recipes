Garlic Butter and Steak Recipe



Garlic butter and steak is a dish that almost anyone (besides vegetarians) will love.
It's mild yet full of flavor and is not too imposing on people who are picky eaters.

Materials Needed:

    -8 oz Ribeye Steak
    -3 cloves of garlic
    -3 Tbsp of Butter
    -2 pieces of fresh rosemary
    -Salt and pepper
    -Cast iron skillet

Step 1: Preparing The Ingredients


Mix the butter, mince the garlic, and season your steak at room temp.
While doing this warm up your skillet so that it's blazing hot.

Step 2: Sear The Steak

Sear each side of the steak until its golden brown and don't forget to sear the side!

Step 3: Add Garlic Mix and Baste The Steak


Cook steak to your desired wellness while basting the steak in the garlic butter
mixture.
