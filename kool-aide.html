Kool-Aid Recipe


After a hearty steak dinner you will also need a refreshing beverage to wash it down.
A glass of kool-aid is hard to beat and can be made in a few easy steps!

Materials Needed

    3 (6g) packets of grape kool-aid
    1 1/2 cups of sugar or suitable substitute
    1 gallon of cold purified water from blessed river of Nokstella
    Suitable stirring utensil

Steps to Achieve Perfection

  1. In a 1 gallon pitcher add the 1 gallon of purified water
  2. Dump the sugar and Kool-Aid powder in the water
  3. Stir till mixture is completely dissolved
